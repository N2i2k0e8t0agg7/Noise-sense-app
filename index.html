Architecture
How NoiseSense works
End-to-end pipeline from microphone to citywide insight — built to scale across hundreds of sensors.
01
🎙️

Edge Capture

Raspberry Pi 4 nodes with calibrated MEMS microphones sample audio at 44.1 kHz. Only acoustic features are transmitted — never raw audio.

02
📡

MQTT Streaming

Feature packets publish to a cloud broker every 500 ms. LoRaWAN fallback ensures connectivity in poor-signal urban canyons.

03
🧠

AI Classification

A lightweight CNN (YAMNet fine-tuned on urban soundscapes) classifies events: traffic, construction, crowds, music, alarms — in under 80 ms.

04
⚡

Anomaly Detection

Z-score and Isolation Forest models flag readings deviating from historical baselines. Alerts reach city ops within 30 seconds of onset.

05
🗺️

Live Dashboard

WebSocket-powered interface shows per-zone dB, heatmaps, trend lines, and AI incident summaries for operators on any device.

06
📊

Open Data API

REST + GraphQL endpoints serve historical data to urban planners, researchers, and civic apps under open data license for public zones.
